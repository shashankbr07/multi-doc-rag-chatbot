1. STRATEGIC VISION
--------------------
Nexus Bank's AI & Technology Strategy is built on three foundational pillars:
  (a) AI-Driven Personalization — deliver hyper-personalized financial products at scale
  (b) Operational Efficiency — automate 60% of manual processes by 2026 using AI/ML
  (c) Risk Intelligence — deploy next-generation risk models that process real-time market signals

Our goal is to become the most AI-native bank in the Asia-Pacific region by FY2027,
reducing cost-to-income ratio to below 40% primarily through technology-driven efficiencies.

2. AI & MACHINE LEARNING INITIATIVES
--------------------------------------

2.1 Credit Risk Scoring (Deployed — FY2024)
- Model: Gradient Boosted Trees + Neural Network ensemble
- Training data: 8 million loan records (2010–2023)
- Performance: AUC 0.94 vs. legacy model AUC 0.81
- Business impact: 30% reduction in default rates for new originations
- Regulatory status: Approved by Credit Risk Committee; model risk review completed

2.2 Customer Churn Prediction (Deployed — Q3 2024)
- Algorithm: XGBoost with SHAP explainability
- Early warning triggered for ~140,000 at-risk customers/month
- Retention campaigns driven by model saved USD 220 million in AUM
- Data sources: transaction patterns, product usage, call center sentiment

2.3 GenAI Customer Support Agent (Deployed — Q4 2024)
- Platform: Google Gemini API + Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Knowledge base: 4,200 internal policy documents, FAQs, product manuals
- Handled 3.2 million queries in FY2024; deflection rate 78%
- Human escalation rate: 22%; average customer satisfaction: 4.4/5
- Languages supported: English, Mandarin, Bahasa, Tamil

2.4 Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Detection (Pilot — Q1 2025)
- Replacing rule-based system with Graph Neural Networks (GNN)
- Dataset: 500 million transactions/month
- Pilot results: 65% reduction in false positives vs. rule-based system
- Expected production deployment: Q3 2025

2.5 Trade Finance Document Processing (In Development)
- Goal: Automate 80% of Letter of Credit (LC) document verification
- Approach: Multimodal LLM (vision + text) to parse shipping docs
- Expected time savings: 8 hours → 15 minutes per LC transaction
- Pilot launch: Q2 2025

3. DATA PLATFORM & INFRASTRUCTURE
-----------------------------------

3.1 Cloud Migration
- Cloud Provider: Amazon Web Services (AWS) — primary; Azure — DR site
- Completed migration: Core banking, data warehouse (310 TB migrated)
- Pending: Legacy payments engine (ETA Q2 2025)
- Cost savings achieved: USD 180 million annually vs. on-premise

3.2 Data Lakehouse Architecture
- Platform: Databricks on AWS
- Data sources: 840 internal + 120 external (market data, macroeconomic feeds)
- Real-time streaming: Apache Kafka — 2.4 million events/second
- Data governance: Unity Catalog; 100% of regulated data classified and tagged

3.3 MLOps Platform
- Tools: MLflow (experiment tracking), Seldon (model serving), Great Expectations (data quality)
- Production models: 47 models currently serving live traffic
- Model monitoring: Automated drift detection with email + Slack alerts
- Average model refresh cadence: 90 days

4. CYBERSECURITY & DATA PRIVACY
---------------------------------
- Zero Trust Architecture rolled out across 95% of the network in FY2024
- Penetration testing: Quarterly external + monthly internal red-team exercises
- SIEM platform: Splunk — processes 850 GB of logs/day
- Data Privacy: Fully compliant with GDPR (EU), PDPA (Singapore), PIPL (China)
- Data residency: All customer PII stored in-country as per local regulations
- Bug bounty program: 120 valid vulnerabilities reported and resolved in FY2024
