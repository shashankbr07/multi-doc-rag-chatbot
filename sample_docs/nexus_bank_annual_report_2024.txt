ANNUAL FINANCIAL PERFORMANCE REPORT — NEXUS BANK PLC
Fiscal Year 2024

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
Nexus Bank PLC delivered robust financial performance in FY2024, achieving a net profit of USD 4.2 billion,
representing a 14% year-on-year increase. The bank's total assets grew to USD 680 billion, supported
by disciplined credit management, diversified revenue streams, and strategic digital transformation initiatives.

Return on Equity (ROE) stood at 16.8%, comfortably above our internal target of 14%. Net Interest Margin (NIM)
improved to 3.45% from 3.21% in FY2023, reflecting the benefit of a rising interest rate environment.

SEGMENT PERFORMANCE
-------------------

1. Retail Banking
   Revenue: USD 9.8 billion (+11% YoY)
   Key drivers:
   - Mortgage originations grew 22% to USD 48 billion
   - Personal loan portfolio expanded to USD 32 billion (NPL ratio: 1.2%)
   - Digital banking active users reached 18 million (up from 13.5 million in FY2023)
   - Mobile payment transaction volume increased 67% to USD 120 billion

2. Corporate & Investment Banking
   Revenue: USD 7.1 billion (+8% YoY)
   Key highlights:
   - Syndicated loan market share rose to 8.4%
   - Advisory fees from M&A transactions: USD 820 million
   - Trade finance volume: USD 95 billion
   - Green bond issuances facilitated: USD 12 billion

3. Wealth Management
   Revenue: USD 2.9 billion (+19% YoY)
   - Assets under Management (AUM) reached USD 210 billion
   - High-Net-Worth client base grew by 15,000 to 142,000
   - Average client portfolio return: 11.3%

4. Treasury & Capital Markets
   Revenue: USD 3.4 billion
   - Fixed income trading: USD 2.1 billion revenue
   - Forex operations: USD 0.8 billion
   - Equity derivatives: USD 0.5 billion

RISK MANAGEMENT
---------------
Credit Risk:
- Overall credit portfolio maintained strong quality with NPL of 1.8%
- Provision Coverage Ratio increased to 82% from 75% in prior year
- Sectors closely monitored: Commercial Real Estate, SME lending

Market Risk:
- Value at Risk (VaR) at 99% confidence: USD 380 million
- Stress test results confirm buffer above regulatory minimums in all scenarios
- Interest Rate Risk in Banking Book (IRRBB): Well within approved limits

Operational Risk:
- Zero material cybersecurity incidents reported in FY2024
- Business Continuity plans successfully tested in Q2 2024
- Third-party vendor risk assessments completed for 450 critical suppliers

TECHNOLOGY & DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION
-------------------------------------
- Core banking system migration to cloud (AWS) completed in Q3 2024 — 30% reduction in IT operational costs
- AI-powered credit scoring model deployed — reduced loan processing time from 72 hours to 4 hours
- Open Banking APIs: 320 live integrations with FinTech partners
- Cybersecurity investment: USD 420 million in FY2024
- GenAI chatbot deployed in contact centers — handled 3.2 million customer queries, 78% deflection rate

OUTLOOK FOR FY2025
-------------------
Management expects revenue growth of 8–12% in FY2025, driven by:
- Continued loan book growth in Retail and Corporate Banking
- Expansion of Wealth Management into Southeast Asian markets
- Further digitization of back-office processes
- Monetization of data & AI capabilities across all business lines

Cautionary factors include potential macroeconomic slowdown, geopolitical uncertainties,
and evolving regulatory requirements under Basel IV.
